# Nurgling Cookbook Pro: A Haven & Hearth Recipe Calculator

**Nurgling Cookbook Pro** is a powerful and intuitive web application designed to help Haven & Hearth players optimize their food choices for character development.

It provides a comprehensive database of 875 recipes, an advanced search engine, and a "Character Engineer" to calculate the expected FEP (Food Event Points) gains based on your character's stats and multipliers.

## Key Features

*   **Advanced Search**: Find recipes by ingredients, stats, name, or even mark your favorites.
*   **Character Engineer**: Input your character's stats and multipliers to see the real FEP gains for each recipe.
*   **Exclusion System**: Exclude ingredients or recipes you don't want to see, and the results will update automatically.
*   **Meal Planner & Shopping List**: Plan your meals and get a shopping list of all the ingredients you need.
*   **Permanent Deployment**: The application is permanently deployed and accessible at any time.
