# Nurgling Cookbook Pro: User Guide

This guide will walk you through the main features of the Nurgling Cookbook Pro and how to use them effectively.

## 1. Character Engineer

The Character Engineer is the core of the application. Here you can input your character's stats and multipliers to get accurate FEP calculations.

*   **Account**: Select your account type (Free, Verified, or Subscribed).
*   **Glut Multiplier**: Enter your glut multiplier.
*   **Table Bonus**: Enter your table bonus.
*   **Realm Bonus**: Enter your realm bonus.
*   **Satiation**: Enter your current satiation level.
*   **Recipe Quality**: Set the expected quality of the recipes.

## 2. Advanced Search

The search bar allows you to find recipes using a powerful query language.

*   **By Ingredient**: `ing:pumpkin`
*   **By Stat**: `str>20%`
*   **By Name**: `name:roast`
*   **Favorites**: `fav:1`

You can combine these queries to narrow down your search.

## 3. Exclusion System

The exclusion system allows you to remove ingredients or recipes from the results.

*   **Exclude Ingredient**: Click on an ingredient in the "INGREDIENTS" panel to move it to the "EXCLUDED" panel. All recipes containing that ingredient will be removed from the results.
*   **Exclude Recipe**: Click on a recipe in the "RECIPES" panel to move it to the "EXCLUDED" panel. Only that recipe will be removed.
*   **Include Item**: Click on an item in the "EXCLUDED" panel to move it back to its original panel.
*   **Exclude/Include All**: Use the "→" and "←" buttons to exclude or include all items at once.

## 4. Meal Planner & Shopping List

*   **Add to Planner**: Click the "+" button next to a recipe to add it to your meal planner.
*   **Shopping List**: The shopping list will automatically update with all the ingredients you need for the recipes in your meal planner.
